We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
